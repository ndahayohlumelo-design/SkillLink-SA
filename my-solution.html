My Proposed Solution: SkillLink SA

SkillLink SA is a digital platform designed to tackle youth unemployment. Users can create profiles, list skills, and connect with job opportunities and training programs. The system matches skills to available jobs, empowering users and helping employers find the right talent quickly.

This platform is scalable, adaptable, and designed to bridge the gap between education and employment. South Africa deserves a system that actually works for its people, and SkillLink SA is the start.
